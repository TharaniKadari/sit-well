How it works

Three steps, no setup

01

Calibrate

Sit however you normally would. Sit Well quietly averages your position for about a second — that becomes your personal target, not some generic ideal.

02

Track

PoseNet follows your eyes and shoulders frame by frame, entirely inside your browser. Nothing is recorded, uploaded, or stored anywhere.

03

Guide

Drift far enough for long enough, and you get a specific nudge — "sit up straight" or "move back to center" — plus a soft chime, not a siren.
