How it works

Three steps, no setup

01

Get ready

Sit the way you normally do. For about a second, Sit Well quietly checks your position — that becomes your own personal goal, made just for you.

02

Track

Your webcam watches your eyes and shoulders, right there in your browser. Nothing is recorded, saved, or sent anywhere.

03

Guide

If you slouch or lean for too long, Sit Well tells you exactly what to fix — like "sit up straight" — with a soft sound and a spoken hint, not a harsh alarm.
